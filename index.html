Thoughts on Web Accesibility. Web accessibility is very important because it ensures everyone has the same level of access. It is also necessary because it stops discrimination. If someone with a disability tries to access your website and it's not disability friendly, it is unfair to them and it can possibly open up a lawsuit. Another example is the colors on a website. If someone is colorblind, they will have a hard time acessing your website. It is important that the website is color blind friendly.
